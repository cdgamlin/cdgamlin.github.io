How to Install IPTV Simple Client in Kodi

  1. Open Kodi and go to Add-ons.
  2. Select My Add-ons and then PVR.
  3. Click on PVR IPTV Simple Client and then Configure.
  4. Under the M3U Playlist URL, enter the URL for your IPTV service.
  5. Click OK and then enable the IPTV Simple Client.
  6. Restart Kodi and the IPTV channels should now be available.
